Friends' trail

The Friends of Mount Edgcumbe Country Park (Registered Charity 295261)

We hope you enjoy this walk. It is intended to showcase the variety of projects that the Friends have supported the park with since 1985.

Without the support of the public, we would not be able to achieve our objective of preserving and enhancing the park for future generations.
